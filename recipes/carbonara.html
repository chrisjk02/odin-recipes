Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Like other Italian classics, eating a bowl of silky, rich, perfectly salty carbonara is life-changing. The classic Roman dish is composed of silky eggs, Sardinian sheep's milk cheese, and al dente Spaghett or spaghettoni is comfort food wrapped around your fork tines. The best part? It's completely possible to make it right at home. Read on for how to make yours the best it can be.
