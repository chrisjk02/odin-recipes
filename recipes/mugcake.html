Chocolate Protein Mug Cake

Description

Sometimes it seems like eating protein is relegated to either shakes or meat â€“ and those two things can get kinda boring. Thankfully, I discovered the joy of mug cakes! You literally put all the ingredients in a mug, microwave it, and bam! You have a delicious dessert!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp oat flour
  • 2 tbsp chocolate protein powder
  • 1.5 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a mug or a ramekin, mix together all dry ingredients.
  2. Add in almond milk, then stir in half the chocolate chips. Add remaining chips on top.
  3. Microwave 60-80 seconds or until mug cake expands to top of mud/ramekin. Check every 10 seconds after 30 seconds.
  4. Sprinkle with sea salt and serve!
